BSLN — Helium Leak Rate Translation (Cryogenic Interface)

Goal: translate leak rates in mbar·L/s into equivalent helium loss in g/day and g/year, assuming low pressure ambient = 1 bar.

Core conversion maths

Given leak throughput Q = 1×10⁻⁸ mbar·L/s.

  1. Convert to SI pressure-volume flow:
    1 mbar·L/s = 100 Pa × 10⁻³ m³/s = 0.1 Pa·m³/s
    Q_SI = Q × 0.1 = 1×10⁻⁹ Pa·m³/s
  2. Ideal gas molar leak at temperature T = 293.15 K:
    ṅ = Q_SI / (R·T) where R=8.314462618 J/(mol·K)
  3. Mass leak for helium M_He = 4.002602 g/mol:
    ṁ = ṅ·M_He
  4. Annualized with availability:
    g/year@99% in 8000 h = (g/h × 8000 × 0.99)
